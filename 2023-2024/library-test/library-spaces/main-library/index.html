Don't forget

Please save your work and start to gather your belongings 15 minutes before the advertised closing time for the building. Staff will begin closing procedures at this time.

Located in the town centre, just off North Street and close to St Salvator's Quad, this is our largest library space.

All University staff and students can use this library.

Entry on the ground floor (called Level 2) is via a ramp. The building has four floors, accessible by stairs. A lift is available.  

  • All University staff and students can browse and borrow the collections using the self-service machines and can use study space in this building.

    • Library Help Desk and IT Services
    • A variety of seats including areas for group study on floors 1 and 2, and individual study options on all floors
    • A dedicated space for postgraduate study is available on Level 1
    • Levels 3 and 4 are silent areas
    • Library search PCs
    • PCs for use
    • Print, copy and scan machines
    • Self-service borrow and return machines
    • Blu-ray and DVD viewing stations
    • Toilets
    • Café - find out more information about University cafés.
    • Lidded drinks as well as snacks and cold foods are permitted on Levels 1 & 2. Lidded drinks are permitted on Levels 3 & 4, but no food is allowed.
